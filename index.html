About Us

Renuva is a manufacturer-first company based in Makati, Philippines, engaged in the production and distribution of eco-friendly household products. We primarily work with business partners, including distributors and resellers, to deliver practical and sustainable solutions for everyday use.

Our operations focus on responsible manufacturing, consistent product quality, and long-term partnerships. Sustainability guides our decisions across all aspects of the business.

Meet Our Team

Mission

To promote the movement towards renewable innovation, committed to delivering quality sustainable products by repurposing waste and educating the community on how we can live a green life through small, powerful conscious choices.

Vision
